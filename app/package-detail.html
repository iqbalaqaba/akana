Tour timeline

  • Pick up at the Airport & Free Program Day 1

    Picked up at the airport, transfer to the hotel to check in. Free program and rest.

  • Barong Dance Ubud & Kintamani Volcano Tour (B,L,D) Day 2

    Breakfast at the hotel, watching the Barong dance performance, followed by Celuk Ubud as a center for silver crafts. and immediately headed towards Kintamani.while enjoying the beauty of the Lake and Mount Batur. The journey continues towards the Tampaksiring temple which is famous for its holy water sources. Back to hotel and take a rest.

  • Tanjung Benoa & Uluwatu Sunset Tour (B,L,D) Day 3

    Breakfast at the hotel, visit the Tanjung Benoa beach of Nusa Dua which is famous for its diversity of water sports activities ( own Cost ). Moving towards the Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park, continued by visiting Uluwatu Temple, the holy temple located at the end of Pecatu hill. Return to the hotel.

  • Free program & Transfer out (B) Day 4

    Breakfast at the hotel, free program until pick-up time arrives. Delivered to the Airport for flights to the next destination.
